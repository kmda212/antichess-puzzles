    How to Play

    1 Select a puzzle from the dropdown and click ▶ Load
    2 Click a piece of your color to select it
    3 Legal moves appear as dots (moves) and red rings (captures)
    4 Click the target square to make your move
    5 The opponent replies automatically if your move is correct
    6 Wrong move? You'll see an error — try a different square
    7 Solve all moves to win! 🏆
